Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Lifescale office ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your Google profile picture

While you use the app, we process:

  • Your avatar position within the virtual office (not stored permanently)
  • Your audio/video status (muted, deafened)
  • Your presence status (Available, Deep Work, On a Call)
  • Workspace membership records stored in our database

How We Use Your Information

  • To identify you within your team's virtual office
  • To display your name and avatar to teammates
  • To remember which workspaces you belong to
  • To enable spatial audio features via LiveKit

Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely in Supabase. Position data is ephemeral and only held in memory while you are connected — it is never written to disk.

Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth — for authentication
  • Supabase — for database and auth
  • LiveKit — for real-time audio
  • Render — for backend hosting
  • Vercel — for frontend hosting

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at the email associated with this project.
